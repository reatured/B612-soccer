B612 Soccer - GMTK Game Jam 2025

A minimalist 2-player soccer game on a circular planet. Defend your goal and kick multiple balls around the looping world in this chaotic interpretation of the "Loop" theme.

Key Features

  • Multi-Ball Chaos - Up to 6 balls simultaneously
  • Circular Planet Physics - Players move freely around the surface
  • Local Multiplayer - WASD vs Arrow Keys
  • Quick Matches - 60-second rounds, first to 5 goals wins
  • Minimalist Style - Clean black/white art with blue space theme

Controls

Player 1: WASD | Player 2: Arrow Keys | ESC: Pause
